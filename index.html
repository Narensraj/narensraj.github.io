01. About Me

I am an Embedded Systems Engineer with hands-on experience building controllers and IoT devices for greenhouses, livestock barns, water-treatment plants, and industrial automation.

My work spans the full stack of embedded development — from hardware bring-up, board-level debugging, and RTOS-based firmware to connectivity, data logging, and integration with dashboards and cloud platforms.

I’ve deployed systems across India, Canada, and the United States and collaborated closely with cross-functional teams (controls, software, and operations) to ship reliable, field-tested solutions.

I enjoy working on:

  • ▹ Real-time control firmware using FreeRTOS and bare-metal C/C++.
  • ▹ Industrial communication using RS-485, Modbus, CAN, and custom protocols.
  • ▹ Secure OTA update pipelines and long-uptime systems for remote sites.
  • ▹ Embedded web dashboards, Node-RED flows, and field diagnostics tools.
